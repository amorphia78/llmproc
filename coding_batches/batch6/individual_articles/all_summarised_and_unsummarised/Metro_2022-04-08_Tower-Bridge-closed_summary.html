Tower Bridge closed after Extinction Rebellion abseils off sides to hang banner

London's Tower Bridge has been shut after eco-protesters hung from ropes and put up a banner saying: 'End fossil fuels now.'

Extinction Rebellion and Just Stop Oil activists took over the bridge earlier this morning before abseiling off the sides and set off flares. Protesters also walked into the middle of the road to stop traffic, leading to huge queues in the capital.

Extinction Rebellion has said it plans to 'get more serious', by creating more roadblocks than ever in central London. 'We won't have pink boats, we won't have pink tables, we will just have people power,' the environmentalist group said.

The 'April Rebellion' is expected to kick off at Speakers' Corner in Hyde Park tomorrow from 10am, with activists aiming to 'disrupt business as usual'.
