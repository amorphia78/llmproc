Just Stop Oil spray-paint English universities orange

PRIMARY: Just Stop Oil activists sprayed orange paint on universities across England including Leeds, Manchester, Cambridge, Bristol, Exeter, Oxford, Falmouth, Sussex, Birmingham and UCL over several days. The protesters targeted university buildings to protest against the UK Government's plans to license new oil and gas projects. Named protesters include recent Leeds graduate Sam Holland (21), Chiara Sarti (24) at Cambridge, philosophy student Harrison (20) at Birmingham, and Oscar (20) at Sussex who wanted to be a teacher. Multiple arrests were made for criminal damage.



SECONDARY: The protests were described as part of "student resistance" with some protesters calling universities "complicit" and urging university leadership to "join students in civil resistance."

OTHER FOCAL: The article mentions the recent approval of the Rosebank oil field, the UK's largest untapped oil field containing up to 300 million barrels of oil.
