Greta Thunberg: Activist arrested at Hague climate protest

PRIMARY: Greta Thunberg joined hundreds of protesters in The Hague attempting to block the A12 highway for the 37th time since 2022. The protest targeted Dutch government fossil fuel subsidies for companies like Shell and KLM. Thunberg and over a hundred demonstrators were arrested after breaking through police lines and sitting on the highway, with Thunberg reportedly arrested twice after returning to the protest.



SECONDARY: The demonstration included a march from the city center with a brass band and protesters singing Dutch songs with climate themes.

OTHER FOCAL: Some newspapers reported local residents were annoyed the protest occurred on the hottest day, potentially disrupting beach access.
