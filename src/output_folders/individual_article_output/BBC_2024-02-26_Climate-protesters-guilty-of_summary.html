Climate protesters guilty of trespass at Wimbledon

PRIMARY: Three Just Stop Oil protesters - Deborah Wilde (69), Simon Milner-Edwards (67), and William Ward (66) - were found guilty of aggravated trespass after storming Wimbledon tennis courts and throwing confetti and puzzle pieces during matches in July. They scaled barriers and disrupted play on Court 18, causing players to appear frustrated and intimidated. The protesters waited for breaks in play but were found to have intentionally caused disruption.
