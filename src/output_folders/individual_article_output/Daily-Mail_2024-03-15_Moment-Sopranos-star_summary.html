Moment Sopranos star Michael Imperioli shoves climate change protesters out of 'An Enemy of the People' Broadway performance

.

PRIMARY: Three Extinction Rebellion environmental activists disrupted a Broadway performance of "An Enemy of the People" by shouting "no theater on dead planet" and walking onto the stage during the second act. One protester, identifying as a theater artist, warned about rising oceans and climate crisis while being escorted out by cast members including Michael Imperioli, who shoved the activist up the stairs while remaining in character.

SECONDARY: No secondary protest actions mentioned.



OTHER FOCAL: Some audience members initially thought the disruption was part of the show since the play addresses environmental concerns. Imperioli later posted on Instagram clarifying he personally supports the cause despite his character's reaction.
