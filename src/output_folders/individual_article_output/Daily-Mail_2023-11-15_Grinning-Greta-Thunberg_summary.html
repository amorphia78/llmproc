Grinning Greta Thunberg is forced to make a run for it as she is mobbed outside court after denying public order offence

Thunberg was charged after a protest in Mayfair in London on October 17.

PRIMARY: Greta Thunberg and 25 other protesters from Fossil Free London blocked the entrance to London's InterContinental Hotel on October 17, preventing access during an oil executives' conference. Some climbed onto the roof and abseiled down while others sat in the road. After 5 hours, police ordered them to move to the pavement but they refused and were arrested.

SECONDARY: Greenpeace members separately rappelled down the building to unfurl a "Make big oil pay" banner during the same protest.
