Just Stop Oil protestors storm airfield 'used by Taylor Swift' and cover planes in orange paint

PRIMARY: Two Just Stop Oil activists, Jennifer Kowalski (28, former sustainability manager) and Cole Macdonald (22), broke into Stansted airport's private airfield at 5am and spray-painted multiple private jets with orange paint using fire extinguishers. They targeted the same airfield where Taylor Swift's jet had landed hours earlier, demanding the UK government commit to ending fossil fuel extraction by 2030. Both women were arrested on suspicion of criminal damage.



SECONDARY: The same group previously targeted Stonehenge with orange powder paint, with two activists (Rajan Naidu, 73, and Niamh Lynch, 21) arrested after members of the public intervened.

OTHER FOCAL: The article focuses on Taylor Swift's connection to the airfield protest, as she was due to perform at Wembley Stadium.
