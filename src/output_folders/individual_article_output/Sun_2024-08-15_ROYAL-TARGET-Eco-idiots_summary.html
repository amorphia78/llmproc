ROYAL TARGET Eco idiots ‘plotting to storm Windsor Castle and break into private royal grounds in three days of chaos’

The protesters will also try and create mayhem in Windsor town centre

PRIMARY: Extinction Rebellion activists are planning to storm Windsor Castle grounds from August 30 to September 1, creating a diversion on The Long Walk to break into private royal grounds while also targeting Windsor town centre hotels, restaurants, and blocking roads. The protest, called "Upgrade Democracy," demands a Citizens' Assembly on Climate and Ecological Justice and targets what they call a "broken and unjust system," though the King won't be present during the demonstration.
