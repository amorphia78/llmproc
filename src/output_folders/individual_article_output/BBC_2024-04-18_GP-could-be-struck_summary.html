GP could be struck off after Just Stop Oil protest

PRIMARY: Dr Sarah Benn, a 57-year-old GP from Birmingham, participated in peaceful demonstrations at Kingsbury oil terminal in Warwickshire in 2022, holding a "no new oil" sign. She breached a civil injunction, was imprisoned for 31 days, and now faces a Medical Practitioners Tribunal that could remove her from the medical register. Her demand was for the government to end new fossil fuel projects.

SECONDARY: Just Stop Oil noted that three GPs are under scrutiny for similar protest activities this year, with Dr Benn's case being the first heard.



OTHER FOCAL: The article focuses on the intersection of medical ethics and climate activism, with Dr Benn arguing her protests align with medical duties to protect public health from climate crisis threats.
