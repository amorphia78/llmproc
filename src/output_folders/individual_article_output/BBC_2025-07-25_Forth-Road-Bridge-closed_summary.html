Forth Road Bridge closed after Greenpeace protest

PRIMARY: Ten Greenpeace activists abseiled off the Forth Road Bridge to protest against the Ineos tanker Independence carrying fracked gas to Grangemouth. The protesters suspended themselves 25m above the shipping lane with red banners, blocking deep water vessel access. They demand governments cut global plastic production by 75% by 2040 and oppose Ineos allegedly sabotaging upcoming UN plastics treaty negotiations.
