Police investigate sabotage claims by UK activists against ‘fossil fuel finance’

PRIMARY: Environmental group Shut the System claimed supporters cut cables and glued electrical cabinets at JP Morgan Chase and Allianz offices, and cut 5G cables at Barclaycard's Northampton HQ. The group demands financial institutions stop fossil fuel financing and adopt climate-aligned targets, threatening escalation by October if ignored. They targeted these companies for being major fossil fuel investors and insurers.

SECONDARY: The group previously sabotaged fibre optic cables in the City in January, causing significant internet slowdowns.
