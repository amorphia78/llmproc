Just Stop Oil activists spray orange paint around Heathrow Airport departure hall

PRIMARY: Two Just Stop Oil activists sprayed orange paint on departure boards at Heathrow Airport's Terminal 5, resulting in their arrest for criminal damage. The protesters were Phoebe Plummer (22) and Jane Touil (58), demanding an end to oil by 2030 as part of the global "Oil Kills" campaign targeting airports worldwide.

SECONDARY: Seven Just Stop Oil activists also staged a protest at Gatwick Airport's South Terminal, sitting down and blocking passengers while wearing orange-colored hand boxes. Eight people were arrested for interfering with public infrastructure.



OTHER FOCAL: The protests are part of an international "Oil Kills" campaign involving 21 groups across 12 countries taking action at 18 airports globally.
