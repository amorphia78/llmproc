Just Stop Oil cover planes in paint ‘hours after Taylor Swift’s private jet arrived’

PRIMARY: Two Just Stop Oil activists cut into Stansted airport's private airfield and sprayed orange paint on private jets using fire extinguishers, targeting planes where Taylor Swift's aircraft are believed parked. The protesters, including 22-year-old Cole Macdonald from Brighton and Oxford student Ms Lynch, demand the UK government commit to ending fossil fuel extraction by 2030. Both were arrested by Essex police.

SECONDARY: The action occurred one day after JSO activists painted Stonehenge orange before the summer solstice, which was condemned by both Rishi Sunak and Keir Starmer as vandalism.
