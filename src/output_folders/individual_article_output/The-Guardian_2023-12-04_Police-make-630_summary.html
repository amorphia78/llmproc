Police make 630 arrests of climate protesters in London in one month

PRIMARY: Just Stop Oil protesters engaged in slow marching along roads as peaceful civil resistance against new oil and gas production. Police made 630 arrests of over 470 people in one month, with almost half arrested under new anti-protest legislation (Public Order Act 2023 section 7). Protesters include students and a 73-year-old who had been previously jailed. Their demand is to stop new oil and gas licensing.

SECONDARY: Some protesters were held for extended periods - a 23-year-old for 56 hours, a 19-year-old student for three nights despite being granted bail, with one considering false imprisonment charges.



OTHER FOCAL: The article focuses on the government's new anti-protest powers and their impact on civil liberties, with Liberty condemning the "staggering and shocking" arrest numbers.

TONE: The article takes a sympathetic tone toward the protesters, emphasizing their peaceful nature and criticizing excessive police powers.
