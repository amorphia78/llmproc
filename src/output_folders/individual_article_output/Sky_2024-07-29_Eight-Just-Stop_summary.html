Eight Just Stop Oil activists arrested at Gatwick Airport after departure gate protest, Sussex Police say

Seven Just Stop Oil activists were seen sitting on the floor inside the airport with their hands inside of lock-on devices. Gatwick Airport says the protestors were being removed from the airport at around 9.15am.

PRIMARY: Eight Just Stop Oil activists were arrested at Gatwick Airport after seven entered the southern terminal at 8am, using suitcases with lock-on devices to block an entrance near a security gate. The protesters sat on the floor with their hands in lock-on devices, disrupting airport operations until being removed around 9:15am. They demanded swift political action on climate breakdown as part of the international "Oil Kills" campaign.
