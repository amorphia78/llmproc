Activists throw soup on Van Gogh painting again

PRIMARY: Three Just Stop Oil protesters (two women and one man) threw orange soup at two Van Gogh Sunflowers paintings at London's National Gallery, hours after two other activists received jail sentences for a similar 2022 attack. The protesters demanded an end to new oil and gas projects, calling imprisoned activists "prisoners of conscience." All three were arrested on suspicion of criminal damage, though the paintings were undamaged.



SECONDARY: In July 2022, two activists had glued themselves to Constable's The Hay Wain at the same gallery.

OTHER FOCAL: The article focuses on the jail sentences given earlier that day - Phoebe Plummer received two years and Anna Holland got 20 months for the October 2022 soup attack.
