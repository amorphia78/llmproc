Bristol grandma climbed M25 gantry 'in climate emergency protest'

PRIMARY: A 76-year-old retired teacher and grandmother, Gaie Delap from Bristol, climbed a motorway gantry on the M25 in November 2022 as part of a Just Stop Oil protest. She is one of 12 protesters accused of breaching a court injunction. The protest affected several junctions across Surrey, London, Essex and Kent, causing considerable delays. The protesters demand the government "stop new oil and gas" and want the climate emergency taken more seriously.
