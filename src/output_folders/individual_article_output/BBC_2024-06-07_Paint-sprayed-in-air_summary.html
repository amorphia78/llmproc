Paint sprayed in air outside duke's wedding

PRIMARY: Two women, aged 69 and 73, from Just Stop Oil sprayed orange powder paint into the air outside Chester Cathedral as the Duke of Westminster and his new wife left after their wedding ceremony. The protesters used devices to project the paint near the entrance as the newlyweds made their way to a car. Both women were arrested on suspicion of public order offences.
