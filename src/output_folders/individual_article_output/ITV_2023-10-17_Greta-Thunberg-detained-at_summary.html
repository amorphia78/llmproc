Greta Thunberg detained at London climate protest

PRIMARY: Greta Thunberg and dozens of other protesters blocked Hamilton Place outside London's InterContinental hotel, which was hosting an Energy Intelligence Forum for oil and gas company executives. The 20-year-old Swedish activist was detained by police, with five people arrested for obstructing a highway. Protesters used banners, pink umbrellas, and smoke flares while chanting "oily money out" and demanding cancellation of the conference, arguing fossil fuel companies deliberately slow renewable energy transition for profit.



SECONDARY: Greenpeace activists separately abseiled from the hotel roof to unfurl a "make big oil pay" banner during the demonstration.
