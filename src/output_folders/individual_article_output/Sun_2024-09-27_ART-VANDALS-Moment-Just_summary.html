ART VANDALS Moment Just Stop Oil eco-idiots throw SOUP over Van Gogh’s painting hours after 2 other activists jailed for same stunt

The paintings were covered just hours after fellow vandals Phoebe Plummer and Anna Holland were sentenced

PRIMARY: Three Just Stop Oil protesters threw soup over two Van Gogh paintings at London's National Gallery today, targeting works in the 'Poets and Lovers' exhibition. This occurred hours after fellow activists Phoebe Plummer (23) and Anna Holland (22) were sentenced to prison for a similar 2022 stunt where they threw Heinz tomato soup over Van Gogh's Sunflowers, causing £10,000 damage to the frame.

SECONDARY: Plummer received additional charges for involvement in a slow march causing travel chaos in west London and for spraying paint on Heathrow Airport departure boards.



OTHER FOCAL: The judge emphasized the cultural value of the artworks and rejected claims of political persecution, stating the protesters "came within the width of a pane of glass of destroying one of the most valuable artworks in the world."
