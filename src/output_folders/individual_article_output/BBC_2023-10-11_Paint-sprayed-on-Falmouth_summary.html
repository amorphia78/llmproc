Paint sprayed on Falmouth University during protest

PRIMARY: A small number of Just Stop Oil protesters sprayed orange paint on two buildings at Falmouth University's Penryn campus on Wednesday. The protest was part of Just Stop Oil's third day of "student resistance" actions. Police were called at midday and the matter is being investigated by both police and the university.

SECONDARY: The day before, Just Stop Oil protesters had similarly daubed paint on a University of Exeter building on Tuesday.



OTHER FOCAL: The university emphasized that while the demonstration began peacefully and they respect lawful protest, it was escalated by the criminal damage caused by a small number of individuals.
