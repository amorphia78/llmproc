Just Stop Oil activists disrupt West End performance of Les Miserables

PRIMARY: Four Just Stop Oil activists stormed the stage during a West End performance of Les Miserables, unfurling their flag and chaining themselves to the stage with bicycle locks. The protesters included 23-year-old community worker Hannah Taylor and 19-year-old student Poppy Bliss. They demanded an end to new oil and gas projects, specifically opposing the government's approval of the Rosebank oil project. The disruption halted the performance for 20 minutes.
