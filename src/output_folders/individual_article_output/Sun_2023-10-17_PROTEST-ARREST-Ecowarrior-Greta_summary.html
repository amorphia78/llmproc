PROTEST ARREST Eco-warrior Greta Thunberg arrested after joining protest outside conference in London

PRIMARY: Greta Thunberg and dozens of other environmental activists protested outside the five-star InterContinental Park Lane hotel, targeting the Energy Intelligence Forum meeting of fossil fuel executives and government ministers. The protesters chanted "oily money out" and attempted to block access to the luxury hotel. Around 21 people were arrested including Thunberg, with charges including obstructing a highway, disrupting public order, and criminal damage.



SECONDARY: Two Greenpeace activists abseiled down from the hotel roof to unfurl a giant banner reading "Make Big Oil Pay."

OTHER FOCAL: The Energy Intelligence Forum has been branded "the Oscars of oil" for bringing together fossil fuel industry leaders and government officials.
